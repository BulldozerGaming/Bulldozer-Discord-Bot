Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Information We Collect

Bulldozer Discord Bot may store the following information required for its operation:

  • Discord Server IDs
  • Discord Channel IDs
  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Role IDs
  • Bot configuration and settings

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Passwords
  • Email addresses
  • Private messages
  • Payment information
  • Discord account credentials

Purpose

Collected information is used solely to provide the bot's features and improve functionality.

Data Sharing

No personal information is sold, shared or transferred to third parties unless required by law.

Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are taken to protect stored information.

Data Removal

Server administrators may request deletion of stored server data by contacting the developer.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Contact
